The 4D Perception Lab is expanding! We are currently seeking a **Machine Learning Researcher** to join our team and contribute to cutting-edge research in spatial-spectral perception systems.

## Position Overview

The successful candidate will work on developing and implementing machine learning algorithms for hyperspectral imaging, 3D reconstruction, and sensor fusion applications, with a focus on defense and security applications.

## Key Responsibilities

- Design and implement machine learning models for spatial-spectral data analysis
- Collaborate with multidisciplinary teams on research projects
- Publish research findings in top-tier conferences and journals
- Contribute to the lab's ongoing research initiatives

## Qualifications

- PhD or Master's degree in Computer Science, Machine Learning, or related field
- Strong programming skills in Python and experience with ML frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow)
- Experience with computer vision and/or hyperspectral imaging is a plus
- Excellent communication and teamwork skills

For more information and to apply, please visit our [LinkedIn job posting](https://www.linkedin.com/jobs/view/4197049156) or contact us directly.

We look forward to welcoming a new team member who shares our passion for advancing perception technologies!
